Who are we? Glad you asked.

We are passionate about derivative support.

For decades, we have been supporting banks for their evaluations and risk analysis of your derivatives. We have been there for every one of your new deals, restructuring and novation. We now want to do the same thing for you and be your derivative support. We want to be your middle office.

We are a group of quants - expert in fixed income derivatives. We are independent of any banks, advisors or vendors. We work for you.

In our view, the days of quarter-end evaluations are over. With all the technology and new ways of doing business, why shouldn’t you know how your trades are doing, every day? Your bank does.

We want to provide you with your P&L, sensitivities and a host of risk analysis every day of the week. We don’t need to know any proprietary information about your trades to do our job. We can provide our reports in a per-notional basis; removing any scale of the trade’s value while still providing detailed reports of your trade’s analysis.
