<div>
    <b>API</b>: price <br><hr>
<b>Summary</b>:It prices a saved trade. The Main useage of this api is to price a trade daily after saving it only once.
<br><br>
<b>Arguments</b>:<br><hr>
<b>As of</b>: This is the date as of when you price the trade for.<br>
<b>Load as</b>: Select a name of trade to be priced. They are created with the field "Save As" populated when pricing a trade.<br>
<br>
