<div>
    <b>API</b>: risk_ladder <br><hr>
<b>Summary</b>:It calculates the spot and ladder sensitivies to all the risk factors of a saved trade.
<br><br>
<b>Arguments</b>:<br><hr>
<b>As of</b>: This is the date as of when you are calculating the sensitivitied for. Note that you can only calculate the sensitivites of the trade for a date whose market data is available.<br>
<b>Load as</b>: Select a name of trade to be proced. They are created with the field "Save As" populated when pricing a trade.<br>
<br>
<b>See Also</b>:<br><hr>
For creating a vanilla swap, see api:price_vanilla_swap<br>
For creating an FX forward, see api:price_fx_forward<br>
